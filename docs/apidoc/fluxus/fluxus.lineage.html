fluxus.lineage#

Products implementing the HasLineage interface are able to store and retrieve the lineage of the intermediary products that were created along the way through the flow to the final product.

Such products can also be tagged with additional labels to help categorize them in the final set of products.

Classes#

HasLineage

Mixin-class for a product of a flow Conduit that is derived from a preceding product.

LabelingProducer

A producer that can label their products.

LabelingTransformer

A transformer that can label its products.

LineageOrigin

A product that has no precursor.
